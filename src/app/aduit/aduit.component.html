SEO Audit & Consultation

Know the steps to improving your SEO performance.

Audits are the cornerstone of successful SEO campaigns. They tell you where your domain performance stands and how to effectively beat your competition.

But you know your clients better than us. By aligning to your strategies and your client’s goals, we can seamlessly consult as an extension of your agency.

Building your SEO blueprint

With our comprehensive approach, we not only optimise your site for technical SEO but also for your target audience. Our Audit and Consultation service is a deep dive into your current SEO performance, diagnosing all parts of your website that require fixes. By conducting a thorough analysis of your site’s content and keyword targeting, we identify opportunities to create new, engaging content that resonates with your target audience and helps drive organic traffic to your site. With the information gathered from the audit, we provide you with an actionable blueprint on how to improve your website’s SEO performance.

There are two layers of service available to you as part of the consultation.

Wave 1

We’ll evaluate your website’s onsite elements, backlinks, keyword targeting, and content. After conducting a thorough analysis, we’ll provide you with a detailed report on the areas that require the most attention and offer practical solutions in a user-friendly format. Our primary objective is to optimise your site for Google, ensure its crawlability, target the right keywords, and safeguard it from penalties.

Wave 2 (optional)

The second wave of our work adds an additional layer of detail to the initial assessment. We’ll tailor our analysis to your site’s specific needs, expanding on the initial assessment to provide a more comprehensive keyword analysis, keyword mapping, backlink audit, content planning, and more. By scheduling this second phase after the first wave, we have the flexibility to allocate all resources towards addressing any significant issues uncovered during the initial evaluation, maximising the impact of your investment.

Tools at our disposal

We possess a comprehensive suite of powerful agency-level software that enables us to delve deep into your website and unearth opportunities for optimisation. Our softwares includes Ahrefs, SEMRush, Sitebulb, Surfer SEO, and Screaming Frog. These tools generate preliminary data that we analyse to pinpoint the crucial sections of your site that require detailed manual auditing.

In addition, we conduct a thorough health check of your Google Analytics and Console accounts to ensure that no vital data is lost due to incorrect setup.

What you’ll receive

Keyword research

We’ll evaluate your existing keyword rankings and important pages before pinpointing appropriate, business-related keywords to concentrate on. By examining the search queries that your competitors are ranking for, but you are not, we can recognise potential keyword opportunities.

Technical SEO auditing

You’ll receive a thorough, step-by-step, and easily actionable technical analysis of the most pressing issues preventing your site from ranking. Our team will conduct a customised analysis that specifically targets your website. We will manually scrutinise the technical aspects of your site to ensure that it meets Google’s guidelines and is optimised for search engine success. We’ll provide detailed analysis and checks on:

  • Keyword cannibalisation analysis
  • Schema markup
  • Sitemap
  • Robots.txt
  • Page architecture
  • Speed optimisation
  • Server Log Analysis
  • Crawlability
  • Meta data analysis
  • Internal Linking
  • Image/video alt tag attribution
  • CTR optimisation
  • Link sculpting
  • Index management
  • Keyword targeting
  • Mobile optimisation
  • Content optimisation
  • Redirect chains
  • 4XX errors

Backlink health check

During the first wave, we’ll conduct a Backlink Health Check to ensure that your website’s backlink profile is not impeding your ranking progress.

Link requirement plan:

To outrank your strongest competitors, we’ll reverse engineer their link profile and assess the volume and quality of links you’ll need. Our link requirement plan will help you build a comprehensive link-building strategy that delivers results.

Backlink audit (Wave 2 Only)

In case we detect any issues with your link profile, we may recommend a comprehensive manual Backlink Audit. This involves a thorough analysis of your link profile to identify any unnatural referring domains/links that could be detrimental to your site’s SEO performance. We’ll also generate a disavow file for you to upload on your Search Console, at your discretion.

Content audit:

We will conduct a thorough analysis of the content on the most crucial pages of your website, identifying any obstacles that may hinder their ranking potential. This includes identifying duplicate or thin content that may result in a penalty. Our team will provide you with recommendations on how to improve your pages and make them more Google-friendly. Additionally, we will evaluate keyword density and keyword cannibalisation and offer detailed explanations on how to optimise your pages for the most effective ranking potential

Content plan:

In the second phase of our services, we will suggest up to 10 content ideas that outline new keyword opportunities for you to target. Our team will reverse engineer your competition in your niche and provide you with content recommendations such as page titles, headings, topics to cover, and suggested content length based on user intent. With our content plan, you’ll have everything you need to create high-quality content that resonates with your audience and drives traffic to your site.
